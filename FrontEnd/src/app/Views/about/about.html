About SpaceShip Taxi

Born and raised on these islands, we know every beach, every hidden cove, and every local dish worth trying.

Who we are?

A local business
with a passion for this island

Space Ship Taxi was founded in 2010 by locals who grew up exploring every corner of Antigua & Barbuda. What started as a small taxi operation has grown into a full-service travel experience combining reliable transport with unforgettable guided island tours. We believe the best way to experience our island is through the eyes of someone who truly loves it. Every driver and tour guide on our team is a local, bringing genuine knowledge, warmth, and pride to every journey.

What Drives Us❗

All our drivers are licensed, background checked, and trained to keep every journey safe.

Our team knows every road and attraction in the area making your tour truly special.

We believe a great ride starts with a warm welcome. Expect a smile every single trip.
