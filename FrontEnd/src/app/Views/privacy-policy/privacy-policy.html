Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: May 14, 2026

Welcome to Spaceship Taxi. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what personal information we collect when you use our website, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. By using our site, you agree to the practices described below.

🗄️

What information we collect

When you use our website to book a ride or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name, so we know who to pick up
  • Your phone number or email address, to confirm your booking
  • Your pickup and drop-off locations, to complete your ride
  • Your requested pickup time and date
ℹ️

How we use your information

We only use your information to:

  • Process and confirm your taxi booking
  • Contact you about your ride (e.g. if your driver is delayed)
  • Improve our service and website

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

📍

Location data

Your pickup and drop-off addresses are used only to plan and complete your journey. We do not track your location in real time through our website, and we do not store your location history after your ride is complete.

🔒

How we protect your data

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure. Access to your personal data is limited to staff who need it to process your booking. However, no website can guarantee 100% security, so please use our service responsibly.

🍪

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to help it function properly (for example, to remember items in a booking form). We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites.

✅

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your data (where applicable)

To make any of these requests, please contact us using the details below.
